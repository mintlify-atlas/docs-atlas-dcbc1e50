## Tips for Wedding Speeches

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep It Concise">
    Wedding speeches should typically be 3-5 minutes long. Focus on quality over quantity, sharing meaningful stories without overwhelming the audience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Balance Humor and Heart">
    Mix lighthearted moments with sincere sentiment. Include funny anecdotes while keeping the overall tone respectful and loving.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Practice Your Delivery">
    Read your speech aloud multiple times before the wedding. This helps you feel comfortable with the content and timing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Personalize the Content">
    Add specific details, inside jokes, and unique stories that only you can share. This makes your speech authentic and memorable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="End with a Toast">
    Conclude your speech by raising your glass and inviting everyone to join you in celebrating the happy couple.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
