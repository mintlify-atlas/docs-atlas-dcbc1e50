## Eulogy Writing Tips

<Warning>
Eulogies require special sensitivity and care. Consider these important points:
- Focus on celebrating life rather than dwelling on loss
- Share positive memories and uplifting stories
- Acknowledge the person's impact on family and community
- Keep religious or spiritual references appropriate to the audience
- Practice your delivery to maintain composure
- Have a backup person ready if emotions become overwhelming
- Aim for 5-7 minutes in length
</Warning>
