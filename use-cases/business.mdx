## Best Practices for Business Speeches

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start Strong">
    Open with a compelling hook: a relevant statistic, thought-provoking question, or powerful story that grabs attention immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Structure Your Content">
    Organize your speech with clear sections: introduction, main points (typically 3-5), and conclusion. Use transitions to guide your audience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Data Wisely">
    Support your arguments with relevant statistics and facts, but don't overwhelm your audience with numbers. Make data memorable and meaningful.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tell Stories">
    Incorporate real examples, case studies, and anecdotes that illustrate your points and make your message relatable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="End with Action">
    Conclude with a clear call to action or key takeaway that your audience can implement immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
