## Common Support Topics

### Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical problems with the speech generator:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the FAQ">
    Visit our [FAQ page](/resources/faq) for answers to common questions and issues.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Try Basic Troubleshooting">
    - Refresh your browser
    - Clear your cache and cookies
    - Try a different browser
    - Check your internet connection
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Report the Issue">
    If the problem persists, report it on our GitHub repository with details about:
    - What you were trying to do
    - What happened instead
    - Your browser and device information
    - Any error messages you received
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Speech Quality Issues

If you're not satisfied with the generated speech:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Provide More Specific Input">
    The quality of your speech depends heavily on your input. Try:
    - Adding more detailed key points
    - Being more specific about tone and style
    - Including relevant names, dates, and context
    - Specifying the desired length more precisely
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Try Different Speech Types">
    Make sure you've selected the most appropriate speech type for your occasion. Different types are optimized for different contexts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Regenerate and Iterate">
    Don't hesitate to generate multiple versions with slightly different inputs until you find one that works well as your foundation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Edit and Personalize">
    Remember that the generated speech is a starting point. Edit it to add your personal touch and make it authentically yours.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
