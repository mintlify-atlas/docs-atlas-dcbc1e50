# Create your first speech

This guide will walk you through the process of generating a personalized speech using SpeechWriter AI. You'll have a polished, professional speech ready in just minutes.

<Note>
SpeechWriter AI is completely free to use. No account creation or credit card required.
</Note>

## Generate your speech

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your speech type">
    Select the type of speech you need from the available options:
    - Wedding toasts
    - Business presentations
    - Keynote addresses
    - Academic speeches
    - Special event speeches (baby showers, eulogies, etc.)
    
    Choose the category that best matches your occasion.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Input your key points">
    Enter the main ideas you want to cover in your speech. Think about:
    - The core message you want to convey
    - Important details about the occasion or subject
    - Any specific stories or anecdotes you want to include
    - Names of people you want to mention
    
    The more specific you are, the more personalized your speech will be.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize tone and style">
    Specify how you want your speech to sound:
    - Formal or casual
    - Humorous or serious
    - Emotional or professional
    - Length preferences
    
    This ensures the AI generates a speech that matches your speaking style and the occasion.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate your speech">
    Click the generate button and let the AI create your personalized speech. The process takes just seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and refine">
    Once generated, review your speech:
    - Read through the content to ensure it captures your message
    - Make any adjustments or edits as needed
    - Practice delivering it to get comfortable with the flow
    
    Your polished, professional speech is now ready to deliver!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
You can generate multiple variations and choose the one that resonates most with you.
</Info>
