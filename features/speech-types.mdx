## Choosing the Right Speech Type

When selecting your speech type, consider these factors:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Occasion and Context">
    Match the speech type to your event. Formal business events require different approaches than intimate family celebrations.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Audience Expectations">
    Consider who will be listening. A keynote address for industry professionals differs from a wedding toast for friends and family.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Speech Purpose">
    Identify your goal—are you inspiring, informing, celebrating, or commemorating? Different speech types serve different purposes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Time Constraints">
    Some speech types naturally run longer than others. Keynote addresses typically require more time than wedding toasts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
Not sure which speech type to choose? Start with the closest match to your occasion, and you can always customize the content further to fit your specific needs.
</Tip>
