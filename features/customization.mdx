SpeechWriter AI gives you full control over your speech content through powerful customization features. Input your own ideas, key points, and preferences to create speeches that truly reflect your message and voice.

## How Customization Works

Our AI takes your input and transforms it into a polished, professional speech while maintaining your core ideas and message. You provide the substance, and we handle the structure and flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Input Your Key Points">
    Enter the main ideas, topics, or messages you want to include in your speech. These form the foundation of your content.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Specify Tone and Style">
    Choose how you want your speech to sound and feel. This ensures the generated content matches your personality and the occasion.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Your Speech">
    Our AI processes your inputs and creates a personalized speech in just minutes, incorporating all your key points.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and Refine">
    Review the generated speech and make any additional adjustments to perfect your delivery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Input Key Points Feature

The key points feature is your primary customization tool. Here's how to make the most of it:

<Accordion title="Be Specific">
Provide detailed information about what you want to say. Instead of "talk about the bride," try "mention how Sarah and I met in college and her dedication to helping others."
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Include Important Details">
Add names, dates, specific anecdotes, or facts you want referenced. The AI will incorporate these naturally into your speech.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Share Your Message">
Clearly state the main message or takeaway you want your audience to remember. This helps the AI focus on what matters most.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Note Special Considerations">
Mention any topics to avoid, special acknowledgments to make, or cultural considerations to respect.
</Accordion>

## Customization Examples

See how customization transforms generic speeches into personalized messages:

### Wedding Toast Example

**Your Input:**
- Met the groom in college, roommates for 3 years
- His dedication to family and career
- How happy we are that he found Emma
- Wish them joy and adventure

**AI Output:** A heartfelt toast that weaves these points into a cohesive, emotional speech with smooth transitions and appropriate wedding toast structure.

### Business Presentation Example

**Your Input:**
- Q4 revenue increased 25%
- Successful product launch in Europe
- Team grew from 50 to 75 employees
- Goals for next quarter

**AI Output:** A professional presentation that highlights achievements, provides context, and builds momentum toward future objectives.

<Tip>
The more specific and detailed your input, the more personalized and relevant your generated speech will be. Don't hold back on the details!
</Tip>
